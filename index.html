About This Project

Rizal Learning Hub - Educational Platform

Educational Purpose

This interactive learning platform was developed to fulfill the requirements of Republic Act 1425 (Rizal Law), which mandates the study of Dr. José Rizal's life, works, and writings in all Philippine educational institutions.
